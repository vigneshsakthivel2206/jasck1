About Lions & Leo Clubs

Founded in 1917, Lions Clubs International is the largest service organization in the world. The Leo Club Program began in 1957 to provide youth with leadership, experience, and opportunity. Leo clubs support communities through volunteerism and skill-building activities worldwide. Leos have access to international opportunities, awards, and a vast network of global impact.

About Us

The Omega Leo Club of Jairams Arts and Science College, Karur, is affiliated with Lions Clubs International District 324-F. We empower youth with leadership and social responsibility through community services and student-led initiatives. Our club is active in eco-awareness, blood donation drives, rural education, and health camps.

We proudly won various awards and honors, including the Best Leo Club Award, Leo President of the Year, and Appreciation Awards from District Governors. All updates and event highlights are actively shared on our Instagram page.
